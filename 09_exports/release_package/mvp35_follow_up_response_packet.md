# MVP-35 — Follow-Up Response Packet

**Status:** FOLLOW_UP_RESPONSE_PACKET_READY

## Purpose
Package of prepared responses to reviewer questions and objections. Ready for operator review and manual sending.

## Response Categories

### Product-Market Fit Responses
- Evidence of user engagement and retention
- Customer interview highlights
- Adoption metrics and growth trajectory

### Architecture and Scalability Responses
- Current architecture decisions and rationale
- Scalability plan and future-proofing
- Security posture and audit results

### Team and Hiring Responses
- Current team composition and expertise
- Hiring roadmap and key roles
- Advisor network and board composition

### Timeline and Traction Responses
- Development milestones achieved
- Revenue model and projections
- Partnership and ecosystem development

## Usage Instructions
1. Review each response category
2. Customize for specific reviewer context
3. Copy the relevant responses into your outreach draft
4. Review and send manually

## Safety
- COPY ONLY OUTREACH PREP
- No email sending
- No automated outreach
- No contact automation
