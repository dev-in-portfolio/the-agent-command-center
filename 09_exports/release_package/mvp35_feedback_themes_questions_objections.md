# MVP-35 — Feedback Themes, Questions, and Objections

**Status:** FEEDBACK_THEMES_QUESTIONS_OBJECTIONS_READY

## Overarching Themes
1. **Product-Market Fit Validation** — Multiple reviewers asked about evidence of PMF, customer adoption, and retention. This is the dominant theme across all reviewer types.
2. **Scalability and Architecture** — Technical reviewers focused on whether the current architecture will scale, database choices, and security boundaries.
3. **Team and Hiring Roadmap** — Recruiters and some investors asked about team composition, key hires, and culture.
4. **Timeline and Traction** — Investors wanted concrete timelines to revenue, milestones, and measurable progress signals.

## Key Questions
- What is your current user retention rate?
- How do you validate product-market fit?
- What is your architecture's scaling strategy?
- What are your key hires in the next 6 months?
- When do you project revenue generation?

## Objections
- "The market seems crowded" — need differentiation narrative
- "Architecture may need rethinking at scale" — need scalability proof
- "Team seems early-stage" — need hiring roadmap and advisor network
