# MVP-35 — Outreach Prep Copy Bank

**Status:** OUTREACH_PREP_COPY_BANK_READY

## Purpose
Collection of copy-ready outreach templates and talking points for manual use by the operator.

## Email Subject Lines
- Following up: Your review of The Agent Command Center
- Thank you for your feedback on our release candidate
- Response to your review questions
- Quick follow-up on your review feedback

## Email Opening Paragraphs
- "Thank you for taking the time to review our public release candidate. Your feedback was invaluable and we have prepared detailed responses to your questions."
- "We appreciated your thoughtful review and wanted to follow up on several points you raised. Below are our responses to your specific questions."
- "Your review raised important questions that deserve thorough answers. We have prepared a response packet addressing each point."

## Talking Points for Calls
- Start with gratitude for their time
- Address their specific feedback directly
- Share any updates since their review
- Ask if they have additional questions
- Discuss next steps and timeline

## Social Media / Message Drafts
- LinkedIn: "Thank you to everyone who reviewed our public release candidate. Your feedback is helping us build a better product."
- Email: "We wanted to personally follow up on your review feedback. Please find our detailed responses below."

## Safety
- COPY ONLY OUTREACH PREP
- No email sending
- No automated outreach
- No contact automation
