# MVP-35 — External Reviewer Reply Guide

**Status:** EXTERNAL_REVIEWER_REPLY_GUIDE_READY

## Purpose
Guide for the operator on how to respond to external reviewer feedback. Provides templates, best practices, and safety notes.

## Best Practices
1. **Personalize each response** — Reference specific reviewer questions, not generic answers
2. **Be transparent** — If you don't have an answer yet, say so honestly
3. **Keep it professional** — Maintain respectful tone regardless of critical feedback
4. **Follow up within 1-2 weeks** — Don't let reviewer momentum cool
5. **Track responses** — Note which reviewers have been contacted and their replies

## Template Structure
1. Thank reviewer for their time and feedback
2. Address their specific questions/concerns
3. Provide requested information or clarification
4. Offer to schedule follow-up discussion if needed
5. Close with appreciation and next steps

## Reviewer-Specific Guidance

### Investors
Focus on: traction, market opportunity, business model, team experience
Avoid: over-promising timelines, speculative revenue projections

### Recruiters
Focus on: team culture, growth opportunities, mission, work philosophy
Avoid: overselling team size, exaggerating hiring progress

### Technical Reviewers
Focus on: architecture decisions, security practices, testing approach, roadmap
Avoid: dismissing valid concerns, oversimplifying complex trade-offs

## Safety
- COPY ONLY OUTREACH PREP
- No email sending
- No automated outreach
- No contact automation
