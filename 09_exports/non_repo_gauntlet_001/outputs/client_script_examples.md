# Client Objection Handling Scripts (Synthetic)

**For training purposes only — not real client interaction scripts.**

### Objection: "insurance is a waste of money"
- Acknowledge: 'Many people feel that way.' Reframe: 'Insurance is about protecting the life you're building, not spending money.' Ask: 'What would happen if your car was in an accident today?'

### Objection: "already have coverage"
- Acknowledge: 'Good — you're ahead of most people.' Reframe: 'Let's check if your coverage keeps up with your current life stage. Things change.' Ask: 'When was the last time you did a full review?'

### Objection: "too many policies already"
- Acknowledge: 'It can feel overwhelming.' Reframe: 'Let me show you how bundling can simplify things and often saves money.' Ask: 'Would 15 minutes to review everything be worth it?'

### Objection: "can't afford another bill"
- Acknowledge: 'Budget is real.' Reframe: 'What would happen if you couldn't work for 6 months?' Ask: 'What's the smallest monthly amount that would give you peace of mind?'

### Objection: "young and healthy, don't need it"
- Acknowledge: 'You're right, you feel great now.' Reframe: 'Insurance is cheapest when you're young and healthy — it's a lock-in, not a bet.' Ask: 'Did you know a $500k term policy at your age is often under $25/month?'

## Unresolved Issues
