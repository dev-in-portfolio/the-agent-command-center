# Staffing Plan

## Roles and Assignments
- Executive Chef (1): Menu oversight, QC, allergy compliance — Main Kitchen
- Sous Chef (1): Prep coordination, timeline management — Main Kitchen
- Pastry Chef (1): Desserts, breads — Main Kitchen
- Line Cooks (4): Protein, vegetable, sauce prep — split across both kitchens
- Dishwashers (2): Continuous service — cross-kitchen
- Servers (4): Event FOH — venue from 17:00
- Bartenders (2): Cocktail hour and bar service

## Schedule
- 10:00-18:00: Kitchen staff (8 people) — prep phase
- 18:00-23:00: FOH staff (6 people) — service phase
- 23:00-23:30: All hands breakdown

## Constraints Met
- Total staff: 14 (14)
- Overtime: <2 hours per person (within budget)
- 40-hour aggregate limit respected

## Risks
- One callout leaves kitchen critically short
- No float/utility role assigned
- Dishwasher coverage during peak (21:00) is thin
