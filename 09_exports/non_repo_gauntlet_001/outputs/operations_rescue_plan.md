# Operations Rescue Plan — Midwest Distribution Partners

## Situation Summary
- 14 orders past due, 22 more at risk
- 6 late orders = 62% of revenue clients
- 1 client (Great Lakes Grocery = 34% revenue) threatening termination
- Warehouse at 60% staffing, WMS upgrade 3 weeks ago causing 91% accuracy
- No client escalation communication has occurred

## Triage: 24-Hour Plan
1. **Immediate (by end of day 1):** Contact Great Lakes Grocery regional director for status call (June 11, 10:00 AM)
2. **By hour 4:** Identify all 14 past-due orders; sequence by revenue and relationship
3. **By hour 8:** Cross-check backordered items against manufacturer availability; prepare substitution list
4. **By hour 12:** Proactively contact all 14 past-due clients with honest status and revised delivery dates
5. **By hour 24:** Update website and email auto-reply to reflect actual (not standard) delivery timelines

## 72-Hour Plan
1. **Days 2-3:** Clear highest-value past-due orders first (revenue sequencing)
2. **Day 2:** Request temp agency RUSH placement (acknowledge 2-week lead time as risk)
3. **Day 2:** Identify 3-5 quick wins from 22 at-risk orders; pull forward what's possible
4. **Day 3:** Begin cross-training 2 dispatchers on WMS picking verification to improve accuracy

## 2-Week Plan
1. **Week 1:** Temp staff onboarding (if agency delivers early)
2. **Week 1:** Implement daily standup (15 min) with Ops Director, Warehouse Lead, and CSR lead
3. **Week 1:** Formal WMS training session for all pickers (half-day)
4. **Week 2:** Target: 96% picking accuracy; clear returns backlog

## Escalation Triggers
- **Red:** Another large client threatens to leave — immediate CEO escalation
- **Yellow:** Picking accuracy below 93% after Week 1 — pause new orders for retraining
- **Orange:** Temp agency cannot deliver in 2 weeks — begin cross-department reassignment

## Risks Not Yet Addressed
- No budget for client credits (may need to offer service recovery even without budget line)
- Owner considering selling — lack of transparency may affect morale if rumors surface
- WMS training gap may take longer than 2 weeks to resolve
