# Back Cover Description

Pip the penguin has polka dots. And she's not happy about it.

In a colony where everyone looks the same, Pip stands out for all the wrong reasons. So she sets off on an epic ocean adventure to find where she truly belongs.

Along the way, she'll need to:
- Survive the beautiful Twilight Zone (it's not scary — it's amazing!)
- Outsmart the mischievous Squid Sisters
- Follow a mysterious glowing fish named Glimmer
- And discover that the thing making her different is her greatest strength

A heartwarming, funny, and dazzling underwater adventure about finding your place in the world — spots and all.
