# Owner/Role Map

## Order Triage & Sequencing
- **Owner:** Operations Director
- **Backup:** Warehouse Lead
- **Decision:** Which orders ship in what sequence

## Client Escalation Communication
- **Owner:** Sales VP (strategic accounts)
- **Owner:** CSR Lead (standard accounts)
- **Backup:** Operations Director
- **Decision:** What to communicate, when, and how

## WMS Training & Accuracy Improvement
- **Owner:** Warehouse Lead
- **Support:** IT (if available) or vendor support line
- **Decision:** Training schedule and go/no-go for accuracy targets

## Temp Staffing Request
- **Owner:** Operations Director
- **Support:** HR (if exists) or owner
- **Decision:** Agency selection and budget approval

## Backorder Identification & Substitution
- **Owner:** CSR Lead
- **Support:** Purchasing (if exists) or vendor contact
- **Decision:** Acceptable substitutions and client communication

## Overtime Approval & Tracking
- **Owner:** Operations Director
- **Limit:** 2 hours/person/week maximum
- **Escalation:** To CEO if exceeded

## Unassigned (Gap)
- System integration between WMS and order management
- Client sentiment tracking
- Long-term staffing strategy
