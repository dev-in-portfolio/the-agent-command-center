# Catering Event Plan: Synthetic Spring Gala 2026
- Date: 2026-06-15
- Venue: Riverside Convention Center, Hall B
- Total guests: 175
- Budget: $12,500

## Dietary Restriction Summary
- Vegan: 12 guests
- Gluten-free: 8 guests
- Nut allergy: 5 guests
- Dairy-free: 7 guests
- Shellfish allergy: 3 guests
- Kosher: 4 guests
- Diabetic-friendly: 6 guests
- Low sodium: 3 guests
- Halal: 5 guests
- Soy-free: 2 guests
- Egg allergy: 3 guests
- FODMAP: 2 guests

## Menu by Service Window

### SW1: Cocktail Hour (18:00-19:00) — Passed Appetizers
- 6 passed appetizer varieties (175 guests × 4 pieces each = 700 pieces)
- Vegan: bruschetta (no cheese), vegetable spring rolls
- Gluten-free: meatballs (GF breadcrumbs), shrimp skewers
- Nut-free: all apps nut-free (pesto replaced with basil oil for nut-allergy guests)
- Shellfish-free alternative: chicken skewers (separate from shrimp)

### SW2: Dinner Service (19:15-21:00) — Plated Dinner
- Main: Herb-roasted chicken breast OR grilled salmon
- Vegan option: Stuffed portobello with quinoa (not stir-fry — avoids soy conflict)
- Kosher: Chicken option plated with separate utensil, no dairy
- Halal: Chicken option, prepared on dedicated flat-top surface
- GF: All sides available GF (roasted potatoes instead of orzo)
- Sides: Roasted seasonal vegetables, mixed green salad (croutons on side)

### SW3: Dessert Buffet (21:00-22:00)
- Flourless chocolate cake (egg allergy: labeled, separate tongs)
- Vegan sorbet trio (nut-free, dairy-free)
- Fresh fruit platter
- Coffee/tea station with dairy-free milk options

### SW4: Late Night Snack (22:00-23:00)
- Mini sliders (GF buns available)
- Veggie cups with hummus
- Cheese board labeled with dairy-free alternatives

## Allergy Conflict Resolution
- **Addressed:** Menu includes pesto pasta (pine nuts) — 5 guests with nut allergy
- **Addressed:** Dessert option includes flourless chocolate cake (contains eggs) — 3 guests with egg allergy
- **Addressed:** Cocktail hour includes shrimp cocktail — 3 guests with shellfish allergy
- **Addressed:** Dinner salad includes croutons (gluten) — 8 guests gluten-free
- **Addressed:** Grazing table includes cheese board — 7 guests dairy-free
- **Addressed:**  'Vegan option' currently listed as 'vegetable stir-fry with tofu' (soy) — 2 guests soy-free
- **Addressed:** Kosher guests cannot have meat and dairy together — seating plate design matters
- **Addressed:** Halal chicken option must use separate cooking surface — only 1 flat-top available in Main Kitchen

## Budget Breakdown
- Labor: $4,200 (target)
- Food: $5,800 (target)
- Equipment rental: $1,500
- Misc: $1,000
- Total: $12,500

## Risk Points
- Late ingredient delivery collapses prep timeline
- Only 4 food processors — all chopping must be sequenced
- Oven capacity: 9 racks total across 2 kitchens — bake times must be staggered
- Halal chicken requires dedicated flat-top — limits Main Kitchen capacity
- Staff count (14) is minimum viable for 175 guests
- Labor budget does not support overtime beyond 2 hours/person

## Budget-Sensitive Alternatives
- Replace salmon with trout ($3-4/lb savings)
- Reduce passed appetizer varieties from 6 to 4
- Replace plated dinner with family-style service (reduces serving staff need)
