# Synthetic Insurance Reasoning Report

**IMPORTANT: This is a synthetic test scenario only.**
No real client data was used. No regulatory advice is provided.
All recommendations are product category suggestions, not specific policy quotes.

## Methodology
- Analyzed 25 synthetic client profiles
- Identified coverage gaps based on life stage, household structure, and income
- Matched gaps to product categories
- Flagged budget sensitivity and common objections

## Key Findings
- Life insurance gap: 4 of 25 clients
- Disability gap: 20 of 25 clients
- Umbrella gap: 3 of 25 clients
- LTC consideration: 6 of 25 clients

## Compliance-Safe Language
- All recommendations are prefixed as 'consider reviewing' or 'evaluate'
- No guarantee of insurability or rate quotes provided
- Clients encouraged to consult licensed agent for actual quotes
- Synthetic profiles may not represent typical risk profiles

## What Could Be Wrong
- Coverage gap detection is rule-based, not actuarial
- No state-specific regulations considered
- No actual underwriting or rate data used
- Objection handling is generalized, not personalized
