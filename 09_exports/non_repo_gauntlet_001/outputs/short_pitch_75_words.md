# Short Pitch (75 words)

Pip the polka-dot penguin doesn't fit in. Her spots make her a target. But when a journey through the deep ocean reveals a world of wonders — including a mysterious glowing fish, a gang of mischievous squid, and an island full of lost treasures — Pip realizes her differences aren't a weakness. They're her superpower. A humorous, heartfelt picture book for ages 3–7.
