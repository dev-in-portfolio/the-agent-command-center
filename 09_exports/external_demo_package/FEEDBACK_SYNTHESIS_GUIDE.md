# Feedback Synthesis Guide — The Agent Command Center

This guide outlines how to combine signals from multiple reviewers into a cohesive product direction.

## Synthesis Goals
- Identify recurring "strongest parts" to double down on.
- Identify recurring "confusing parts" to refactor or re-explain.
- Prioritize the "Biggest Risks" identified by technical stakeholders.
- Select the most requested "Next Steps" for the roadmap.

## Avoiding Biases
- **No Over-weighting:** Do not let one loud reviewer override the consensus of three others.
- **No Fabrication:** Synthesize only from received, authentic feedback packets.
- **Role Balance:** Ensure technical signals and product signals are both represented.

## Decision for MVP-20
The synthesis round concludes with a formal recommendation for the MVP-20 goal. This ensures that the next phase of development is directly informed by external reviewer signal.
