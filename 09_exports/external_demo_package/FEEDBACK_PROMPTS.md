# Feedback Prompts — External Review

We appreciate your feedback! Please consider the following questions during your review:

## Product & Value
1. Is the problem of "uncontrolled agentic workflows" clear and compelling?
2. Does the "request control layer" concept feel like an effective solution?
3. Which feature demonstrated (intake, activity tracking, safety gates) feels most critical?

## Security & Trust
1. Does the "Safety Boundary" (no browser secrets, RLS) provide confidence?
2. Is the "Zero-Persistence" token handling clearly explained and valuable?
3. Are the intentionally blocked actions (Approve/Execute) seen as a strength or a limitation?

## Clarity
1. What was the most confusing part of the demo or architecture?
2. Are the briefs (Technical, Recruiter, Founder) targeted correctly?
3. What one thing would make the product pitch stronger?

## Technical
1. Is the browser-to-Netlify-to-Supabase proxying clearly understood?
2. Does the tiered validation discipline provide confidence in system integrity?
