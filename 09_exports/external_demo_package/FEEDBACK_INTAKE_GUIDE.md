# Feedback Intake Guide — External Review

Thank you for agreeing to review **The Agent Command Center**. Your feedback is critical to ensuring our "safety-first request control layer" meets the needs of modern agentic workflows.

## Purpose
The goal of this feedback round is to validate the product positioning, security boundaries, and demo experience before we enable approval and execution automation.

## Reviewer Personas
Please select the persona that best matches your context:
- **Technical Reviewer:** Focus on architecture, RLS, proxying, and safety gates.
- **Recruiter:** Focus on engineering discipline, product thinking, and demo narrative.
- **Founder & Operator:** Focus on intake visibility, audit trails, and roadmap.
- **Product Reviewer:** Focus on problem/solution fit and feature prioritization.

## What to Include
- Honest impressions of the product concept.
- Specific feedback on the "Safety Boundary."
- Identification of confusing parts or technical blockers.
- Suggestions for the next 2-3 MVP phases.

## What NOT to Include
- **DO NOT** include any bearer tokens.
- **DO NOT** include Supabase secret keys or database passwords.
- **DO NOT** include private infrastructure details or credentials.

## How to Return Feedback
1. Fill out the [Reviewer Response Form](./REVIEWER_RESPONSE_FORM.md).
2. Generate the "Feedback Packet" in the dashboard or copy the text manually.
3. Return the text packet via email or the agreed-upon channel.
