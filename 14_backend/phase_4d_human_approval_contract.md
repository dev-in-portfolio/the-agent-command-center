# Phase 4D: Human Approval Contract

## Principles
1. **No Implicit Approval**: Page visits, hovers, or navigation must never trigger a system action.
2. **Explicit Consent**: Every sensitive action must have a deliberate click and confirmation.
3. **Traceability**: Approval must include Actor ID, assumed Role, Action ID, and Timestamp.
4. **Non-Repudiation**: Approvals are logged in the immutable audit ledger.
5. **Multi-Step**: High-risk actions require a separate confirmation phrase or MFA (Multi-Factor Auth).

---
*Note: This contract defines the behavior for future interactive phases.*
